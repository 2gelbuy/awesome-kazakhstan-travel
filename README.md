> A curated guide to traveling in Kazakhstan: cities, nature, culture, food, and practical tips.

Kazakhstan is the world's ninth-largest country and Central Asia's most accessible destination. This guide covers everything from visa-free entry for 90+ countries to remote canyons that rival the Grand Canyon.

## Essential Info

- **Visa:** [90 days visa-free](https://about-kazakhstan.com/kazakhstan-visa-free-countries/) for 77 countries (EU, US, UK, Canada, Australia, Japan, South Korea)
- **Currency:** Kazakhstani Tenge (KZT). 1 USD = ~470 KZT (2026)
- **Languages:** Kazakh (state), Russian (widely spoken), English (in tourist areas of Almaty/Astana)
- **Best time:** June-September for hiking, December-March for skiing
- **Safety:** [Low crime rate](https://about-kazakhstan.com/is-kazakhstan-safe-for-travel/), safer than most of Eastern Europe (Global Peace Index)
- **Budget:** $30-50/day backpacking, $80-150/day mid-range

## Nature & Outdoors

Kazakhstan has everything from 7,000m peaks to the Caspian Sea shore.

- [Charyn Canyon](https://about-kazakhstan.com/charyn-canyon/) - the "Grand Canyon of Central Asia" (80km from Almaty)
- [Kolsai Lakes](https://about-kazakhstan.com/kolsai-lakes/) - three alpine lakes at 1,000-2,700m
- [Big Almaty Lake](https://about-kazakhstan.com/big-almaty-lake/) - turquoise lake at 2,511m (30 min drive from Almaty center)
- [Mangystau](https://about-kazakhstan.com/mangystau/) - otherworldly desert landscapes on the Caspian coast
- [Kazakhstan Mountains](https://about-kazakhstan.com/kazakhstan-mountains/) - Tien Shan, Altai, Karatau
- [National Parks](https://about-kazakhstan.com/kazakhstan-national-parks/) - Altyn-Emel, Ile-Alatau, Burabay
- [Lake Balkhash](https://about-kazakhstan.com/lake-balkhash/) - half fresh water, half salt water
- [Kazakhstan Hiking](https://about-kazakhstan.com/kazakhstan-hiking/) - trails and routes
- [Skiing & Shymbulak](https://about-kazakhstan.com/shymbulak/) - ski resort at 3,200m
- [Medeu](https://about-kazakhstan.com/medeu-almaty/) - world's highest skating rink (1,691m)
