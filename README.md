## Essential Info

- **Visa:** [90 days visa-free](https://about-kazakhstan.com/kazakhstan-visa-free-countries/) for 77 countries (EU, US, UK, Canada, Australia, Japan, South Korea)
- **Currency:** Kazakhstani Tenge (KZT). 1 USD = ~490 KZT (2026)
- **Languages:** Kazakh (state), Russian (widely spoken), English (in tourist areas of Almaty/Astana)
- **Best time:** [Best Time to Visit Kazakhstan](https://about-kazakhstan.com/best-time-to-visit-kazakhstan/) - June-September for hiking, December-March for skiing
- **Safety:** [Low crime rate](https://about-kazakhstan.com/is-kazakhstan-safe-for-travel/), safer than most of Eastern Europe (Global Peace Index)
- **Budget:** [$30-50/day backpacking](https://about-kazakhstan.com/kazakhstan-budget-travel/), $80-150/day mid-range
- **eSIM:** [eSIM for Kazakhstan](https://about-kazakhstan.com/esim-kazakhstan/) - stay connected without local SIM cards
